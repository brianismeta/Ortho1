Pong for the Orthoverse
February 2023 Tournament

 

PLAYERS:
1. John Rigler
2. Keir Finlow-Bates
3. Ken Newton
4. Mitko Dimitrov

Rules:
 
 
    • 1. There will be TWO ROUNDS,
      2. First round is “semi-final elimination”
      3. FANGWANINA will play SORALFROMANCE in the first round
      4. OLWABARBIA will play PENORBDEL in the first round
      5. It will be a best of 5 games (first one to win 3 games is the winner of the round)
      6. I don’t think there will be necessarily a “home advantage”, but we will alternate the games like this:
      7. First game: Home (FANGWANINA / OLWABARBIA) vs Visitor (SORALFROMANCE / PENORBDEL). Home team creates the game, Visitor joins the game.
      8. Second game: Home (FANGWANINA / OLWABARBIA) vs Visitor (SORALFROMANCE / PENORBDEL).
      9. Third game: Home (SORALFROMANCE / PENORBDEL) vs Visitor (FANGWANINA / OLWABARBIA)
      10. Fourth game (if necessary): Home (SORALFROMANCE / PENORBDEL) vs Visitor (FANGWANINA / OLWABARBIA)
      11. Fifth game (if necessary): Home (FANGWANINA / OLWABARBIA) vs Visitor (SORALFROMANCE / PENORBDEL)
      12. Note that each individual game ends when one side scores 5 points. The screen will freeze and show the winning side with 5 points as green color, and the other side as red color. Please record the score and take a screenshot. Although this is based on “honor” system, we would like to be able to list the individual game scores for posterity.

      A recording may look something like this:

      FANGWANINA (Home) 5 – SORALFROMANCE (Visitor) 3
      FANGWANINA (Home) 4 – SORALFROMANCE (Visitor) 5
      SORALFROMANCE (Home) 4 – FANGWANINA (Visitor) 5
      SORALFROMANCE (Home) 3 – FANGWANINA (Visitor) 5
      FANGWANINA won the round 3 games to 1.

      Note this is just an example, Mitko will never lose on home turf!
      13. Note that if technical issues (like the one I experienced with Ken on Monday) cause an issue with following these rules to the letter, the combatants can ideally agree to compromise on playing the series in a way that is fair so the outcome can be certified by both.
      14. The first round can start any time that the combatants agree upon themselves. The schedule is for them to determine because everyone is on different time zones and have their own busy schedule.
      15. The window of playing the first round is between (inclusive) Wednesday February 8 and Saturday February 11. All the games can be played in a row in one session, or the combatants can play on a different schedule of their choosing if they want to play in multiple sessions.
      16. Please report the scores / screen caps soon after playing a game.
      17. After the first round ends, there will be two players left, who won their individual round.
      18. The second (and final) round between these two players will be played between Monday February 13 and Thursday February 16).
      19. The home player for the final round (in my opinion yields no advantage) will be determined based on the strongest player in the first round – first by the fewest number of games played to decide, and if it is a tied, based on the score differential (points for subtract points against) and if tied, then coin flip between the two players on honor system.
      20. The home vs visitor will follow the same pattern as the first round.
      21. Please report the scores / screen caps soon after playing a game.
